Today's Pause — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026
Last Updated: April 23, 2026

Today's Pause (the "App") respects your privacy and follows applicable privacy and data protection requirements. This policy explains what information is stored when you use the App and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. There is no sign-up or login process, and we do not request your name, phone number, email address, or similar information.

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Stored ItemPurposeStorage Location
Quick self-check result categoryDelivering personalized messagesOn device (AsyncStorage)
Bookmarked message IDsBookmarks featureOn device
History of viewed messagesPreventing duplicate deliveryOn device
Notification settings (on/off, time)Daily one-line deliveryOn device
BGM settings (on/off, theme)Background music preferenceOn device
Onboarding completion statusSkipping onboarding on returnOn device

2. Device Permissions

The App uses the following permissions, with each purpose described below.

  • Notifications: Sends local notifications when the "one line a day" feature is enabled. You can turn this off at any time in settings.
  • Network: Not currently used. May be used in future updates to refresh app content.

The App does not collect or access your camera, photos, microphone, contacts, or location.

3. When Using Share Features

When you use share features (such as copying text, or sharing via KakaoTalk or Instagram), only the screen image or message text you choose is passed to other apps based on your selection. The App does not separately log or store who you shared with or what you shared.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all data is stored only on your device, there is no externally retained data on our side.

You can delete all data stored on your device — categories, bookmarks, notification settings, BGM settings, viewed-message history, and onboarding records — at any time through Settings → "Reset Data". If you delete the App, this data is also removed from your device at the same time.

5. Third-Party Sharing and Outsourcing

The App currently does not share user information with third parties and does not outsource data handling to external entities.

6. Possible Future Changes

The following features may be added in the future to improve service quality or usability. When they are added, this policy will be updated and you will be notified within the App.

  • Analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase): Aggregating in-app usage patterns (screen views, events) — no personally identifiable information is collected
  • Advertising (e.g., Google AdMob): Use of non-identifying advertising identifiers (such as ADID)

When changes occur, we will notify users at least 7 days in advance through in-app announcements or update notes.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not primarily intended for children under the age of 14, and we do not intentionally collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has entered their personal information, we will delete it without delay.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • The right to view, modify, and delete the data stored on your device at any time (exercised through in-app settings or by deleting the App)
  • The right to submit inquiries regarding this policy or how your data is handled

9. Security Measures

All data is stored only on your device, protected by the operating system's standard safeguards within the app's sandbox.
